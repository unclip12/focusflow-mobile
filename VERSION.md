# FocusFlow Mobile — Version History

---

## v1.5.0 — 2026-02-28

### What's New

- **Revision Hub Overhaul** — Connected ALL study resources to the revision hub
  - FA pages & subtopics → revision tracking with SRS scheduling
  - Sketchy Micro/Pharm videos → revision items created when watched
  - Pathoma chapters → revision items created when watched
  - UWorld wrong questions → automatically queued for revision
  - Knowledge Base entries → merged into unified hub
- **Source Filtering** — Filter by FA, Sketchy Micro/Pharm, Pathoma, UWorld, or KB
- **SRS Scheduling** — All revision items now use proper spaced repetition (strict mode: 12 steps, 8h → 180 days)
- **Rich Revision Cards** — Source icon badge, revision step (R0 of 12), due time, last studied info
- **3-Tab Layout** — Due Now / Upcoming (7 days) / All revisions
- **Version System** — App version shown in Settings with changelog

### What Needs Testing

- Toggle a Sketchy/Pathoma item as watched → verify revision appears in hub
- Log a UWorld session with wrong answers → verify revision appears
- Mark items as revised → verify SRS step advances
- Check filter chips work correctly
- Verify version info in Settings > About
